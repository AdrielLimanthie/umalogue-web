# Umalogue

A catalogue and organiser for your Umamusume: Pretty Derby veteran data.

**Live app:** https://umalogue.app

---

## What is this?

Umalogue lets you browse, filter, compare, and organise your trained veteran (trained character) data from Umamusume: Pretty Derby. Import your data, and filter & sort your veterans with advanced filter options for an easier time organising your veteran list.

## Getting Your Data

To export your veteran data from the game, use [UmaExtractor](https://github.com/xancia/UmaExtractor) — a third-party tool that reads local game data from your device.

> **Use at your own risk.** UmaExtractor is not affiliated with this project. I make no guarantee of the safety or legality of using the tool to extract data from the game. You are solely responsible for how you use it.

## Data & Privacy

All your veteran data is stored **locally in your browser** (via localStorage) — nothing is sent to or stored on any server. Your data stays entirely on your device.

The trade-off: your data is only accessible from the **same browser and device** you imported it on. Switching browsers, clearing site data, or using a different device means starting fresh.
